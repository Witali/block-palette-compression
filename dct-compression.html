Addressable DCTBS2 layout

Each independent 4:2:0 MCU stores one 16×16 luma transform at low rates or four 8×8 luma transforms at high rates, plus two 8×8 chroma transforms. An optional clustered library supplies one directly addressed DCT prototype per component; no component depends on a neighboring MCU.
